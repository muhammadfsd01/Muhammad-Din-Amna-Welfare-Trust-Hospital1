Our Mission

We aim to provide excellent medical facilities to all underprivileged and needy individuals, ensuring no one suffers due to lack of treatment. We also support patients under the Sehat Sahulat Program. Our hospital is certified by the Punjab Healthcare Commission and the Pakistan Centre for Philanthropy.
